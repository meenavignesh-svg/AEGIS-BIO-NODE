AEGIS BioNode

Modular Gyroid Perfusion Bioreactor with Sterile Optical Sensor Docking Interface

Project Overview

The AEGIS BioNode is an advanced modular perfusion bioreactor designed for high-fidelity cell culture. By utilizing a Vascular-Mimetic Gyroid Perfusion Scaffold, the system ensures optimal nutrient transport and low-shear environments, critical for tissue engineering applications.

Core Innovations

  • Septum-Lock Sterile Interface: Allows for reusable optical sensor modules to dock securely without compromising the sterile boundary of the bioreactor cartridge.
  • Distributed Sensing Network: Enables spatial culture state reconstruction by mapping metabolic data from multiple sensor nodes across the lattice.
  • Modular Design: Enables rapid iteration and sterile component replacement, reducing cross-contamination risks common in traditional stirred-tank systems.
